About Me

Stefano Picker is a world-class Product Manager with 15 years of experience in Italy, the United Kingdom and both coasts of the United States.

He holds an MBA with Honors from a World's Top 50 Business School, but he loves to go beyond the strategic level and delve into product requirements and user stories, prototyping, iterative development, testing and trials.

Stefano is a student, practitioner and advocate of Design Thinking, Agile Development and The Lean Startup. He is equally comfortable hiring and coaching a team of product managers at a Fortune 50 company, or being a solo entrepreneur to develop a new cutting-edge IoT product with a global team of freelancers.

He is now enhancing his technical skills at the Penn University Coding Bootcamp, to support his latest entrepreneurial venture and feed his passion for great user experience!
